# Why `@0xhq/beaut` ?

## The problems

Building crypto products often starts with a long, familiar checklist:

- trim `addresses` for UI,
- collapse transaction `hashes`,
- format `balances` with the right `locale` and precision,
- turn raw block/tx data into `explorer links`,
- validating `bigint` forms,

... and the list goes on.

Every time, teams end up `reinventing the wheel`, writing their own validation,
deciding which locales to support, and patching edge cases behind the scenes.
That repetition leaves you less time for the actual product and more for arguing
about whether the hash should show 6 or 8 characters.

## The Beaut way

`Beaut` is a response to that `fatigue`. Instead of shipping every repo with a bespoke
suite of small utilities, Beaut gathers those indispensable helpers into a single,
dependable `toolbox` that already validates inputs, applies sane defaults, and exposes
a predictable surface across base utilities, currency formatters, explorer link
builders, number helpers and more.
